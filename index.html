Crystal Collector!


Instructions:

You are given a random number to begin the game.

Each of the 4 crystals is assigned a random value to begin the game but the value is a secret.
When you click on a crystal, its value is added to your total.

You must match your total to the target number exactly to win the game.

The target number and crystal values will reset at the beginning of each game.
